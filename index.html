For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar to dissovle. Halve all of the figs and toss in the sauce pan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frquently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Asdd salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a samll pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is comppletely dissovled and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in th egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozenm basin and process according to manufacturer instructions, i,e., let spin and thicken for 20minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/-10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup marscapone
1/2 tsp pink salt
1 lb Black Mission Figs
1/2 cup brown sugar
2-4 tbsp water
1 1/2 heavy cream
1/3 cup granulated sugar
2 egg yolks
1 lemon, juiced
2 tbsp butter
1 cup honey roasted pecans, roughly chopped
